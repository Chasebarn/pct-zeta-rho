Phi Chi Theta is a national professional business fraternity connecting students, alumni, and industry professionals across the country. The organization focuses on leadership development, business education, and career preparation.

Zeta Rho Chapter — University of South Florida

Ranked #3 nationally through the Next National Chapter evaluation, recognizing sustained excellence across professional development, philanthropy, and chapter operations.

How our chapter operates.

Philanthropy gives members the opportunity to lead initiatives, volunteer in the community, and create measurable impact. Through fundraising and service events, members develop leadership experience while supporting organizations such as the Hillsborough Education Foundation, JA Finance Park, and Parkland Cares.

Professionalism equips members with the skills and exposure needed to succeed in the business world. Through guest speakers, structured development programs, workshops, and events like our Fall Summit, members strengthen their communication, networking, and career readiness.

Social brings members together through chapter traditions and events such as intramural teams, our Fall Formal, Spring Retreat, and other chapter-hosted gatherings throughout the semester. These experiences allow members to connect outside of meetings, strengthen relationships within the chapter, and build a network that extends beyond the classroom.

Phi Chi ThetaZeta Rho · USF

Phi Chi Theta’s Zeta Rho Chapter was established at the University of South Florida in September 2018 by 18 founding business students. Since its founding, the chapter has focused on preparing members for internships and full-time roles through structured professional development, service, and chapter standards. Today, PCT continues to grow as a professional organization that balances career outcomes, philanthropy, and an active chapter culture.
