Common questions.

Do I need to be a business major?

Rush is open to business and economics majors and minors. If business is part of your academic path at USF, you’re eligible.

I’m a freshman / senior — is it too early or too late?

Neither. Rush is open to all class years, and every class brings something different to the chapter.

Is rush a high-pressure evaluation?

No. Our process is built on mutual fit — six structured events where you get to know us as honestly as we get to know you. No prior experience with Greek or professional organizations is required.

How do I start?

Submit the interest form and follow @pctusf on Instagram — event dates, locations, and details for the semester are announced there.

Phi Chi ThetaZeta Rho · USF

Phi Chi Theta’s Zeta Rho Chapter was established at the University of South Florida in September 2018 by 18 founding business students. Since its founding, the chapter has focused on preparing members for internships and full-time roles through structured professional development, service, and chapter standards.
